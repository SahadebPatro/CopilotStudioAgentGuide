Microsoft Copilot Studio · Enterprise Learning Framework

Your complete guide to
building agents at scale.

This framework is a free, open educational resource for everyone learning to design, build, test, and operate enterprise agents in Microsoft Copilot Studio. Whether you're a first-time maker or an enterprise architect, everything you need is here — organized, opinionated, and practical.

Start with the Getting Started roadmap below to understand the journey, then explore any of the eight framework pillars at your own pace.
